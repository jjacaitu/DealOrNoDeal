Welcome to Deal Or No Deal!

There are 20 briefcases in the game board, each one containing a different amount of money ranging from $1 to the highest amount which is $2,000,000. You will have to chose one of the 20 briefcases as yours at the start of the game, which you will hold.


In each round you will be asked to open a fixed number of briefcases and the amount inside each of them will be revealed! after that you will recieve a call from the banker, who will make you an offer. You will have 15 seconds to decide if you make a deal with the banker or not! if you make a deal all the remaining briefcases will be opened one by one and finally the amount you had in your briefcase will be known, letting you realize if you made a good deal or not! If you decide to continue playing you will have to pick more briefcases to open and the cycle will continue.

The objective:

The main goal of the player is to get the highest amount possible or at least try to make a good deal at the end!
